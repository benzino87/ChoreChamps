Welcome to Chore Champs!

An easy-to-use chore tracker for children! influenced by a web app that monitors progress for agile development team sprints applied at home! Parents will now have an easy way to assign and distribute chores, and the ability to track their children's progress on a daily/weekly/monthly basis. Kids will have a very intuitive application that uses a points based system for rewarding their hard work.
